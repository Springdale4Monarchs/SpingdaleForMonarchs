Springdale for Monarchs is a community group fighting to save the monarchs in Springdale, Arkansas. Our goal is to create a butterfly-friendly environment for migrating monarchs. Home monarch gardens, monarch waystations, and microbeds along the Razorback Greenway will make Springdale “the place” to be every September as we come alive with beautiful Monarchs.

By using this mobile app you can help Springdale for Monarchs know where milkweed needs to planted along the Razorback Greenway in Springdale, Arkansas. You can also use the interactive map to locate milkweed and most importantly Monarchs!
